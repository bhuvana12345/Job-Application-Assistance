PROMPT: TAILOR MY RESUME FOR THIS JOB
======================================
Copy this entire prompt. Paste into Claude.ai.
Fill in the two sections marked below.
Takes about 90 seconds per job.

======================================

I am applying for a job. Please help me tailor my resume keywords without changing my actual achievements.

JOB DESCRIPTION:
[PASTE THE FULL JOB DESCRIPTION HERE]

MY CURRENT RESUME BULLETS:
[PASTE YOUR CURRENT BULLET POINTS FROM WORD HERE]

MY PROFILE (for context):
[PASTE YOUR profile.json HERE — or just paste the skills and experience sections]

Please do the following:

STEP 1 — Extract keywords
List the top 12 ATS keywords and phrases from this job description.
Focus on: technical skills, tools, methodologies, and any specific phrases they repeat.

STEP 2 — Gap check
Put a checkmark next to keywords already in my bullets.
Put an X next to keywords that are missing.

STEP 3 — Rewrite only the changed bullets
For each MISSING keyword, rewrite only the one bullet point where it fits most naturally.
Rules:
- Do NOT invent new achievements
- Do NOT add skills I didn't list in my profile
- Use the EXACT phrasing from the job description where possible (ATS matches exact strings)
- Keep the bullet the same length as the original
- If a keyword genuinely doesn't fit anywhere, flag it as "cannot add honestly"

STEP 4 — Show me only what changed
Output ONLY the bullet points that changed. Show:
  BEFORE: [original line]
  AFTER:  [new line]

STEP 5 — Score
Keyword match before: X/12
Keyword match after: Y/12

Keep it tight. I will copy the AFTER lines directly into Word and replace them.
